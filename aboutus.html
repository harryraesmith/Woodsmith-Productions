About Us


Harry Rae Smith is a graduate of the Manchester School Of Art Filmmaking course. He started making short stop-motion animations at a young age, and quickly discovered a love of storytelling throughout his development as a filmmaker. His favourite movie is Heat, and he makes a mean mug of tea. His Instagram can be found here.






James Woodall is also a graduate of the Manchester School Of Art Filmmaking course. Having grown up with dyslexia, he connected quickly with the audio-visual medium of film. His love of genre films and creature-features have fuelled his creative journey since then. His favourite movie is The Thing, and he mixes a mean White Russian. His Instagram can be found here.
